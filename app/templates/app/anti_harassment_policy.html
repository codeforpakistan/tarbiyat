Ensuring a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for all

Purpose

This policy aims to ensure a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment free from sexual harassment for all students, faculty, staff, and visitors at GPGC Mansehra and within the Tarbiyat Internship Platform.

Scope

This policy applies to:

  • All students, faculty, staff, mentors, and third parties (e.g., vendors, contractors) associated with the platform.
  • All platform-related settings: virtual meetings, internship sites, events, field activities, online communications, emails, and social media interactions.
  • All participating educational institutions and partner organizations.

Definition of Sexual Harassment

Sexual harassment includes, but is not limited to:

1. Unwelcome Advances

Repeated or inappropriate expressions of romantic/sexual interest.

2. Inappropriate Communication

Written or verbal communication of a sexual nature: comments, jokes, or messages with sexual content (in person or digitally).

3. Unwanted Physical Contact

Physical conduct of a sexual nature: unwanted touching, gestures, or physical contact with sexual intent.

4. Quid Pro Quo

Request for sexual favors: demands or suggestions for sexual acts in exchange for academic, internship, or professional benefits.

5. Sexually Demanding Attitude

Persistent sexual attention, innuendo, or pressure, especially in power-imbalanced relationships.

6. Academic/Work Interference

Harassment affecting a person's ability to study, work, or participate fully in internship activities.

7. Hostile Environment

Conduct that creates fear, discomfort, or emotional distress due to its sexual nature.

8. Retaliation

Punishing or mistreating someone for rejecting advances or filing a complaint.
