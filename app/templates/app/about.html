About Tarbiyat

Tarbiyat is the government's official internship management platform, designed to connect institute students with meaningful professional opportunities across Pakistan.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between academic learning and practical experience by providing a comprehensive platform that facilitates quality internship placements for students across Pakistan.

Key Features

  • Verified company and institute partnerships
  • Streamlined application process for students
  • Comprehensive progress tracking and evaluation
  • Teacher and mentor collaboration tools
  • Government oversight and quality assurance

Our Community

Tarbiyat serves students, teachers, mentors, and government officials, creating a collaborative ecosystem that ensures quality internship experiences and meaningful professional development opportunities.
