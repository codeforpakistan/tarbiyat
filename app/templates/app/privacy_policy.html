Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Information We Collect

When you use Tarbiyat, we collect information that you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Educational information (institute, program, academic records)
  • Professional information (company details, work experience)
  • Application and internship progress data

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Facilitate internship placements and applications
  • Enable communication between students, mentors, and teachers
  • Track internship progress and provide evaluation tools
  • Improve platform functionality and user experience
  • Ensure compliance with government education policies

Information Sharing

We share your information with relevant parties for internship purposes:

  • Student information with potential mentors and companies for applications
  • Progress reports with teachers and educational institutions
  • Aggregated data with government education departments
  • Information required by law or for platform security

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • File complaints with relevant data protection authorities

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@tarbiyat.gov.pk
