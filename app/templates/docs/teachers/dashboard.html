Complete guide to navigating and using your teacher dashboard effectively

Dashboard Components

Welcome Header

  • Personal greeting: Shows your title and full name
  • Institution info: Your institute name and department
  • Quick context: Current academic period information

Statistics Cards

Total Students

Students under your supervision

Active Internships

Administrative Controls

If you're an administrative contact, you'll also see:

  • Edit Institute: Manage institutional details
  • Organization Requests: Review registration requests
  • User Management: Oversee teacher and student accounts

Quick Actions

📝 Submit Report

Create weekly progress reports for students

  • Academic progress assessment
  • Skill development tracking
  • Mentor coordination notes

👀 Review Reports

Check student and mentor submissions

  • Student self-assessments
  • Mentor evaluations
  • Progress comparisons

📜 Issue Certificate

Generate completion certificates

  • Internship completion certificates
  • Academic credit documentation
  • Achievement recognition

Understanding Your Data

All data shown on your dashboard is filtered to show only information relevant to your role and permissions. Make sure you're logged in with a teacher account and have necessary permissions.
