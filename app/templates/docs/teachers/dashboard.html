👨‍🏫 Teacher Dashboard Guide

Complete guide to navigating and using your teacher dashboard effectively

Dashboard Overview

The teacher dashboard is your central hub for monitoring student internships, tracking academic progress, and managing institutional oversight responsibilities.

Your Role: Academic supervisors who monitor students during internships, coordinate with mentors, and ensure educational objectives are met.

Accessing Your Dashboard

📍 Navigation

  1. Log in to your teacher account
  2. Click "Teacher Dashboard" in the main navigation
  3. Or access directly via URL: /teacher/dashboard/

⚠️ Prerequisites: You must complete your teacher profile before accessing the dashboard. If your profile is incomplete, you'll be redirected to create it.

Understanding Your Data

📊 Statistics Interpretation

High Active Internships: Indicates strong industry partnerships and student engagement
Low Report Submission: May require follow-up with students and mentors
Performance Trends: Help identify successful programs and areas for improvement

⚠️ Key Metrics to Monitor

  • Submission Rate: Aim for 90%+ weekly report compliance
  • Performance Distribution: Monitor students needing support
  • Completion Rate: Track successful internship completions
  • Mentor Feedback: Ensure positive industry relationships

Dashboard Best Practices

✅ Recommended Actions

  • Check dashboard daily during internship periods
  • Review student progress weekly
  • Respond to alerts promptly
  • Coordinate with mentors regularly
  • Document academic assessments
  • Monitor institutional metrics

❌ Common Pitfalls

  • Ignoring overdue report notifications
  • Delayed response to student issues
  • Poor communication with mentors
  • Inconsistent progress monitoring
  • Missing completion deadlines
  • Inadequate documentation

Troubleshooting

Dashboard not loading properly

Check your internet connection and refresh the page. Ensure your teacher profile is complete.

Missing student data

Verify that students are properly enrolled in your institute and have active profiles.

Incorrect statistics

Statistics are updated in real-time. If numbers seem wrong, contact support for data verification.

Access denied errors

Ensure you're logged in with a teacher account and have necessary permissions.
