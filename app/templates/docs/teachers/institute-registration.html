Complete guide to registering your educational institute on the Tarbiyat platform

What is Institute Registration?

Institute registration allows educational institutions to join the Tarbiyat platform as official academic partners. This enables student supervision, academic coordination, and internship management.

üéØ Why Register Your Institute?

  • ‚Ä¢ Official recognition on the platform
  • ‚Ä¢ Enhanced visibility for students and companies
  • ‚Ä¢ Access to institutional analytics and reporting
  • ‚Ä¢ Ability to coordinate with multiple teachers

How to Register Your Institute

Step 1: Access Registration Form

  1. Log into your teacher dashboard
  2. Navigate to "Organization Requests" section
  3. Click "Request New Institute Registration"
  4. Complete the registration form with accurate details

Step 2: Submit Registration Request

Fill out all required information:

  • Complete institute name and address
  • Official contact information
  • Academic verification details
  • Email domain for verification
  • Detailed description of programs offered
  • You'll receive confirmation of submission
  • Track request status via "Organization Requests" page

Approval Process

What Happens Next?

1

Pending Review: Your request is submitted and awaiting official review

2

Official Review: Platform officials verify documents and institute details

3

Approval: Institute is created and you become the admin contact

Reviews typically take 3-5 business days. You'll be notified via email when your request status changes.

Institute vs Company Registration

üìù Key Difference

Institutes are academic institutions (universities, colleges) that supervise students during internships. Companies are business organizations that provide internship opportunities and mentorship.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Can't edit institute after approval

Only the teacher who registered the institute (admin contact) can edit institute details. Other teachers need to request changes through the admin.

Students can't join my institute

Ensure your institute has domain verification enabled and students are using emails from the correct domain. Check that the institute status is approved.
