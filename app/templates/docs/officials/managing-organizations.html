Troubleshooting Common Issues

Organization not appearing in search results

Check all filters and search terms. Some organizations may be filtered out by status or verification filters.

Cannot approve/reject organization

Verify you have appropriate authority level and approval permissions. Some organizations may require higher authority levels.

Domain verification not working

Ensure the organization has set a valid email domain and that you have verification permissions.

Large number of pending approvals

Use filters to prioritize by submission date, organization type, or authority level. Consider batch processing similar organizations.
