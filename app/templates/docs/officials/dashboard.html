Troubleshooting &Support

Dashboard loading slowly

Large datasets may cause slower loading. Use filters to narrow down results and improve performance.

Statistics don't match expectations

Ensure you're viewing the correct date range and that all filters are properly applied. Statistics update in real-time.

Cannot access certain management functions

Check your authority level and approval permissions. Contact system administrator if access should be granted.

Need to generate reports

Use the export functionality in management views or contact technical support for custom report generation.
