Application Status Overview

Once you submit an application, it goes through different stages. Understanding these statuses helps you know what to expect and when to take action.

‚è≥ Pending

What it means: Your application is submitted and under review by the mentor.

What to do: Wait patiently. Most reviews take 5-7 business days.

‚úÖ Accepted

What it means: Congratulations! You've been selected for the internship.

What to do: Check your email for next steps and internship start instructions.

‚ùå Rejected

What it means: Your application was not selected this time.

What to do: Don't get discouraged. Apply to other positions and improve your profile.

üîÑ Active Internship

What it means: You're currently participating in the internship.

What to do: Submit regular progress reports and engage actively with your mentor.

Application Management Actions

üîÑ Withdrawing Applications

When you can withdraw:

  • Application status is "Pending"
  • You've accepted another position
  • Your circumstances have changed

How to withdraw: Click "Withdraw" button on the application and confirm your decision.

üìß Following Up

When to follow up:

  • More than 7 business days with no response
  • Application deadline has passed
  • You have additional relevant qualifications

How to follow up: Send a polite email to the contact person listed in the position details.

Progress Reports During Internships

üìä What Are Progress Reports?

Progress reports are regular updates you submit during your internship to track your learning, document achievements, and communicate with your mentor and academic supervisor.

üìÖ Reporting Schedule

  • Frequency: Typically weekly or bi-weekly (set by your mentor)
  • Deadline: Usually due by end of week or reporting period
  • Reminders: System sends notifications when reports are due
  • Late submissions: May affect your internship evaluation

üìù What to Include in Progress Reports

Required Sections:

  • Tasks completed this period
  • Skills learned or developed
  • Challenges faced and solutions
  • Goals for next period
  • Overall satisfaction rating

Additional Tips:

  • Be specific about accomplishments
  • Include metrics where possible
  • Mention collaboration and teamwork
  • Ask questions or request feedback
  • Keep it professional but personal

üöÄ Submitting Progress Reports

  1. Navigate to your dashboard during an active internship
  2. Click "Submit Progress Report" or check notifications
  3. Fill out all required sections thoughtfully
  4. Review your report before submitting
  5. Submit before the deadline
  6. Check for mentor feedback on previous reports

üéì With Academic Supervisor

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Application status hasn't changed after a week

This is normal. Some mentors take longer to review applications. If it's been over 10 business days, consider sending a polite follow-up email.

Can't submit progress report

Ensure all required fields are filled. Try refreshing the page or using a different browser. Contact support if the issue persists.

Mentor not responding to communications

Try different communication channels (email, phone if provided). If unresponsive for over 3 business days, contact your academic supervisor or platform support.

Want to change internship after acceptance

Contact your mentor and academic supervisor immediately. Changes after acceptance can affect your reputation and may not always be possible.

Success Tips

  • Stay Organized: Keep track of all your applications in a spreadsheet if needed
  • Set Reminders: Use calendar reminders for progress report deadlines
  • Be Professional: All communications reflect on your professional image
  • Document Everything: Keep records of important communications and achievements
  • Seek Feedback: Actively ask for feedback to improve your performance
  • Network: Build relationships that extend beyond the internship period
  • Reflect and Learn: Use progress reports as opportunities for self-reflection
