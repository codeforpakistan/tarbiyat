Learn how to search and apply for internships

Browsing Available Positions

  1. Visit the "Browse Positions" page from your dashboard or navigation menu
  2. Use filters to narrow down positions by location, company, or requirements
  3. Click on any position to view detailed information
  4. Check application deadlines and requirements carefully

Applying for Positions

To apply for an internship position:

  1. Ensure your profile is complete with updated resume
  2. Click "Apply" on the position details page
  3. Write a compelling cover letter specific to the position
  4. Review your application before submitting
  5. Track your application status from your dashboard

Application Requirements

Before Applying, Make Sure You Have:

  • Complete student profile with academic information
  • Updated resume (PDF, DOC, or DOCX format)
  • Valid institute affiliation
  • Meet the position's year of study requirements

Tips for Success

  • Apply early - don't wait until the deadline
  • Customize your cover letter for each position
  • Highlight relevant skills and coursework
  • Follow up professionally if you don't hear back within a week
  • Maintain a professional email address and communication style
