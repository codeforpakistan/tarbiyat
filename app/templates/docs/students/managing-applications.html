Managing Active Internships

Once your application is accepted and you start your internship:

  1. Regular progress reports will be required (frequency set by mentor)
  2. Keep track of your learning objectives and achievements
  3. Communicate regularly with both your mentor and academic supervisor
  4. Submit all required documentation on time

Progress Reports

Important: Progress reports help track your learning and development during the internship.

Progress reports typically include:

  • Tasks completed during the reporting period
  • Skills learned and knowledge gained
  • Challenges faced and how they were overcome
  • Goals for the next reporting period
  • Overall satisfaction and feedback

Communication Best Practices

  • Respond to mentor communications promptly
  • Ask questions when unclear about expectations
  • Keep your academic supervisor informed of progress
  • Report any issues or concerns immediately
  • Maintain professional communication at all times
