Dashboard Overview

Your student dashboard is your central hub for managing internship applications, tracking progress, and staying updated on opportunities. It provides a personalized view of your internship journey.

Quick Access: Navigate to your dashboard anytime by clicking "Dashboard" in the top navigation menu.
