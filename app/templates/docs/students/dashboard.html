🎓 Student Dashboard Guide

Learn how to navigate and use your student dashboard effectively

Dashboard Overview

Your student dashboard is your central hub for managing internship applications, tracking progress, and staying updated on opportunities. It provides a personalized view of your internship journey.

Quick Access: Navigate to your dashboard anytime by clicking "Dashboard" in the top navigation menu.

Dashboard Sections

📊 Quick Stats

At the top of your dashboard, you'll see:

  • Number of applications submitted
  • Pending applications awaiting review
  • Active internships (if any)
  • Available positions matching your profile

📝 Recent Applications

View your most recent applications with:

  • Position title and company name
  • Application date
  • Current status (Pending, Accepted, Rejected)
  • Quick action buttons (View, Withdraw)

🎯 Recommended Positions

Positions suggested based on your profile:

  • Matches your year of study requirements
  • Aligns with your field of study
  • Fits your skills and interests
  • Application deadlines are still open

🔔 Recent Activity

Stay updated with:

  • Application status changes
  • New position notifications
  • Progress report reminders
  • Important announcements

Quick Actions

🔍 Browse Positions

Find new internship opportunities

Redirects to the position browser with filters

📋 View All Applications

Manage your application history

Shows complete application timeline

⚙️ Edit Profile

Update your information

Keep your profile current and complete

📊 Progress Reports

Submit internship updates

Available during active internships

Profile Completion Status

⚠️ Important: Complete Your Profile

Your dashboard will show completion status for:

  • Basic information (name, email, contact)
  • Academic details (institute, student ID, major, GPA)
  • Resume upload
  • Skills and portfolio information

Note: Incomplete profiles cannot apply for positions.

Dashboard Tips

  • Check Daily: New opportunities are posted regularly
  • Update Regularly: Keep your profile information current
  • Respond Promptly: Act quickly on application updates
  • Use Filters: Recommended positions are tailored to your profile
  • Track Deadlines: Pay attention to application closing dates
  • Stay Organized: Use the applications page to manage multiple submissions
