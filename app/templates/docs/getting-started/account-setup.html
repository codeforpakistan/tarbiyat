Get started with your Tarbiyat account

Creating Your Account

  1. Visit the registration page and provide your email address
  2. Choose a secure password
  3. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox
  4. Complete the profile setup process

Choosing Your Role

Important: Your role cannot be changed after selection. Choose carefully based on your primary function.

Select the role that best matches your function:

  • Student: If you're looking for internship opportunities
  • Mentor: If you represent a company offering internships
  • Teacher: If you're an academic supervisor from an educational institute
  • Official: If you're a government administrator overseeing the program

Email Domain Verification

For verified organizations, your email domain must match the organization's official domain. This ensures institutional integrity and prevents unauthorized access.

Example: To join "University of Example", your email must be from "@university.edu.pk"
