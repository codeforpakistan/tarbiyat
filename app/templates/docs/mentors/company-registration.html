Complete guide to registering your company on the Tarbiyat platform as a mentor

Overview

As a mentor, you can register your company on the Tarbiyat platform to start offering internship positions to students. The registration process involves submitting your company details for official review and approval.

Registration Process

1

Access Registration Form

  • Navigate to your mentor dashboard
  • Click "Register Organization" or access via direct URL: /register-organization/
  • Select "Company" as organization type
2

Complete Company Information

  • Company Name: Official registered name
  • Industry: Select relevant industry sector
  • Description: Brief overview of company activities
  • Address: Physical business address
  • Website: Company website URL (optional)
  • Contact Email: Official company email
  • Phone: Business contact number
  • Email Domain: Company email domain for verification
3

Upload Required Documents

  • Registration Certificate: Official company registration document
  • Authorization Letter: Letter authorizing you to represent the company
  • Ensure documents are clear and readable
  • Supported formats: PDF, DOC, DOCX, JPG, PNG
4

Submit for Review

  • Review all information for accuracy
  • Submit registration request
  • You'll receive confirmation of submission
  • Track request status via "Organization Requests" page

Approval Process

What Happens Next?

1

2

3

Managing Your Requests

  2. Navigate to "Organization Requests" section
  3. View current status of your registration request

Request Status Types

Pending

Request submitted and awaiting review

Approved

Company created and ready to use

Rejected

Request declined with feedback

Troubleshooting

"Access denied" when trying to register

Ensure you have a complete mentor profile and the can_register_organization permission. Contact support if the issue persists.

Document upload fails

Check file size (must be under 10MB) and format (PDF, DOC, DOCX, JPG, PNG). Ensure files are not corrupted.

Request was rejected

Check the official feedback in your request details. Address the concerns and submit a new request with corrected information.

Can't edit company after approval

Only the mentor who registered the company (admin contact) can edit company details. Other mentors need to request changes through the admin.
