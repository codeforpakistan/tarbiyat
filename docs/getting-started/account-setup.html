Profile Completion

Why Complete Your Profile?

A complete profile:

  • Increases Visibility: Better matching with opportunities
  • Builds Trust: Verified profiles get more attention
  • Improves Experience: Personalized recommendations
  • Enables Features: Access to all platform functionality

Profile Components

Personal Information

  • Profile Photo: Upload a professional headshot
  • Contact Details: Phone number and address
  • Bio/Summary: Brief description of your background
  • Languages: Languages you speak fluently

Role-Specific Information

For Students:

  • Educational institution
  • Field of study and major
  • Year of study and expected graduation
  • GPA and academic achievements
  • Skills and competencies
  • Resume/CV upload
  • Portfolio links (if applicable)

For Mentors:

  • Company/organization affiliation
  • Job title and department
  • Years of experience
  • Areas of specialization
  • Industry certifications

For Teachers:

  • Educational institution
  • Department and faculty position
  • Teaching subjects/areas
  • Research interests
  • Publications (if applicable)

For Officials:

  • Government department/agency
  • Official position and responsibilities
  • Areas of oversight
  • Contact information

Security Settings

Password Security

  1. Strong Passwords
    • Use at least 8 characters
    • Include uppercase and lowercase letters
    • Add numbers and special characters
    • Avoid personal information
  2. Password Updates
    • Change passwords every 90 days
    • Don't reuse old passwords
    • Use unique passwords for each account

Privacy Controls

  • Profile Visibility: Choose who can see your profile
  • Contact Information: Control access to your contact details
  • Application History: Manage visibility of your applications

Account Verification

Email Domain Verification

  • Students: Verify using your institutional email
  • Mentors: Use your company email domain
  • Teachers: Verify with your institutional email
  • Officials: Use your government email address

Document Verification

Upload required documents:

  • Students: Student ID, enrollment certificate
  • Mentors: Company ID, authorization letter
  • Teachers: Faculty ID, employment letter
  • Officials: Government ID, authorization

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Login Problems

Can't remember password?

  1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
  2. Enter your email address
  3. Check email for reset link
  4. Follow instructions to create new password

Account locked?

  • Contact platform support
  • Provide account verification details
  • Wait for administrator review

Email Issues

Not receiving emails?

  1. Check spam/junk folder
  2. Add tarbiyat@gov.pk to contacts
  3. Verify email address is correct
  4. Contact support if issues persist

Email already in use?

  • You may already have an account
  • Try logging in instead
  • Use "Forgot Password" if needed
  • Contact support for account recovery

Profile Problems

Can't upload documents?

  • Check file size (max 5MB)
  • Use supported formats (PDF, DOC, JPG, PNG)
  • Ensure stable internet connection
  • Try a different browser

Institution not listed?

  • Contact your institutional coordinator
  • Request institution verification
  • Provide official documentation
  • Allow 5-7 business days for approval

Getting Help

Support Channels

  1. Documentation: Check our comprehensive guides
  2. FAQ Section: Common questions and answers
  3. Email Support: support@tarbiyat.gov.pk
  4. Help Center: In-platform support system

Response Times

  • General Inquiries: 24-48 hours
  • Technical Issues: 4-8 hours
