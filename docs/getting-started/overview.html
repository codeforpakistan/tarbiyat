🇵🇰Platform Overview

Welcome to Tarbiyat, Pakistan's comprehensive government internship management platform. This system connects students, educational institutions, government organizations, and private companies to facilitate meaningful internship experiences.

🎯What is Tarbiyat?

Tarbiyat (تربیت) means "training" or "education" in Urdu. Our platform serves as the central hub for managing government-sponsored internship programs across Pakistan, ensuring transparency, efficiency, and quality in the internship process.

Key Features

🎓For Students

  • •
    Profile Creation: Build comprehensive academic and skill profiles
  • •
    Internship Discovery: Browse and search available positions across government departments and partner organizations
  • •
    Application Management: Apply to multiple positions and track application status
  • •
    Progress Tracking: Monitor internship progress with regular reports

🏫For Educational Institutions

  • •
    Student Oversight: Monitor your students' internship applications and progress
  • •
    Institute Management: Manage your institution's profile and verification status
  • •
    Academic Supervision: Provide academic guidance and evaluation for interns

🏢For Organizations & Companies

  • •
    Position Management: Create and manage internship positions
  • •
    Application Processing: Review and process student applications
  • •
    Intern Management: Track and evaluate intern performance
  • •
    Progress Monitoring: Generate reports on intern development

👮For Government Officials

  • •
    System Administration: Oversee the entire internship ecosystem
  • •
    Verification Management: Verify and approve educational institutions and companies
  • •
    Policy Implementation: Ensure compliance with government internship policies
